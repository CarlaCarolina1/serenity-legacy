# 🔍 Google Search Console Setup Guide

**For:** Serenity Legacy Real Estate Website  
**Time Required:** 15-20 minutes  
**Difficulty:** Easy (step-by-step guide)

---

## 🎯 **WHY SET UP GOOGLE SEARCH CONSOLE?**

- ✅ **Free** - No cost
- ✅ **See Search Performance** - Which keywords you rank for
- ✅ **Submit Sitemap** - Helps Google index your site faster
- ✅ **Find Errors** - See if Google has trouble accessing pages
- ✅ **Monitor Indexing** - See which pages are indexed
- ✅ **Track Clicks** - See how many people click from Google

---

## 📋 **STEP-BY-STEP SETUP**

### **STEP 1: Go to Google Search Console**

1. Open your web browser
2. Go to: **https://search.google.com/search-console**
3. Sign in with your Google account
   - Use the same account you used for Google Business Profile (if possible)

---

### **STEP 2: Add Your Property**

1. Click **"Add property"** button
2. Choose **"URL prefix"** (recommended)
3. Enter your website URL:
   ```
   https://serenitylegacy.net
   ```
4. Click **"Continue"**

---

### **STEP 3: Verify Ownership**

Google needs to verify you own the website. You have several options:

#### **Option A: HTML File Upload (Easiest - Recommended)**

1. Click **"HTML file"** tab
2. Download the HTML file Google provides
3. I'll help you upload it to your website
4. Click **"Verify"**

**I can do this for you!** Just let me know when you get to this step.

#### **Option B: HTML Tag (Alternative)**

1. Click **"HTML tag"** tab
2. Copy the meta tag Google provides
3. I'll add it to your website's `<head>` section
4. Click **"Verify"**

**I can do this for you!** Just send me the tag.

#### **Option C: Domain Name Provider (If you have DNS access)**

1. Click **"Domain name provider"** tab
2. Follow instructions to add TXT record to your DNS
3. Click **"Verify"**

**Note:** This requires access to your Squarespace DNS settings.

---

### **STEP 4: Submit Your Sitemap**

Once verified, submit your sitemap:

1. In the left menu, click **"Sitemaps"**
2. Under **"Add a new sitemap"**, enter:
   ```
   sitemap.xml
   ```
3. Click **"Submit"**

**What this does:** Tells Google all the pages on your website so it can index them faster.

---

### **STEP 5: Request Indexing (Optional but Recommended)**

Help Google index your important pages faster:

1. In the left menu, click **"URL Inspection"**
2. Enter your homepage URL: `https://serenitylegacy.net`
3. Click **"Test Live URL"**
4. If it says "URL is not on Google", click **"Request Indexing"**
5. Repeat for important pages:
   - `/properties`
   - `/services`
   - `/about`
   - `/contact`

---

## 📊 **WHAT YOU'LL SEE IN SEARCH CONSOLE**

### **Performance Tab**
- **Total Clicks:** How many people clicked from Google
- **Total Impressions:** How many times your site appeared in search
- **Average Position:** Your average ranking position
- **Click-Through Rate:** Percentage of people who clicked

### **Coverage Tab**
- **Valid Pages:** Pages Google successfully indexed
- **Errors:** Pages with problems
- **Warnings:** Pages with minor issues

### **Sitemaps Tab**
- Shows if your sitemap was successfully processed
- Lists all pages Google found

### **URL Inspection**
- Check if specific pages are indexed
- Request indexing for new/updated pages

---

## 🎯 **WHAT TO DO AFTER SETUP**

### **Week 1:**
- Check that sitemap was processed (should show all your pages)
- Request indexing for important pages
- Check for any errors in "Coverage" tab

### **Week 2-4:**
- Check "Performance" tab (may not have data yet)
- Monitor "Coverage" for any new errors
- Fix any errors that appear

### **Month 2+:**
- Review "Performance" to see which keywords you rank for
- See which pages get the most clicks
- Identify opportunities to improve

---

## 📈 **UNDERSTANDING YOUR DATA**

### **Good Signs:**
- ✅ Sitemap shows all pages indexed
- ✅ No errors in Coverage
- ✅ Impressions increasing over time
- ✅ Clicks increasing over time

### **Things to Watch:**
- ⚠️ Errors in Coverage - Fix these
- ⚠️ Pages not indexed - Request indexing
- ⚠️ Low click-through rate - Improve titles/descriptions

---

## 🆘 **TROUBLESHOOTING**

**Problem:** Can't verify ownership
- **Solution:** Try HTML file upload method (I can help with this)

**Problem:** Sitemap shows errors
- **Solution:** Check that sitemap.xml is accessible at `https://serenitylegacy.net/sitemap.xml`

**Problem:** No data in Performance tab
- **Solution:** Normal! It takes 1-2 weeks for data to appear. Your site needs to be indexed first.

**Problem:** Pages not indexing
- **Solution:** Request indexing for each page manually, or wait (can take days/weeks)
