## 💡 **PRO TIPS**

1. **Be Consistent** - Post regularly (3-5 times per week)
2. **Engage** - Respond to comments and messages quickly
3. **Mix It Up** - Vary your content types
4. **Use Stories** - Instagram/Facebook Stories for behind-the-scenes
5. **Track Performance** - See which posts get the most engagement
6. **Be Authentic** - Show your personality and expertise
7. **Include CTAs** - Always tell people what to do next (call, visit website, etc.)
