## 📋 **STEP-BY-STEP SETUP**

### **STEP 1: Go to Google Business Profile**

1. Open your web browser
2. Go to: **https://business.google.com**
3. Click **"Manage now"** or **"Get started"**

---

1. Sign in with your Google account (or create one if needed)

### **STEP 4: Verification**

Google will ask you to verify your business. You have 3 options:

**Option A: Phone Verification (Fastest)**
- Google will call you
- Answer and enter the code they give you
- ✅ **Recommended** - Usually instant

**Option B: Email Verification**
- Google sends email to your business email
- Click the verification link
- ✅ **Recommended** - Usually instant

**Option C: Postcard Verification**
- Google mails a postcard to your address
- Takes 5-7 business days
- Enter the code from the postcard

**Choose the fastest option available!**

---

Use this text:
```
Expert real estate services for buying properties, selling properties, land sales, new residential homes, commercial real estate, and customizable homes in Orlando, Clermont, and Central Florida. Specializing in luxury properties, investment opportunities, and personalized service.
```

#### **C. Add Photos**
- **Logo:** Upload your Serenity Legacy logo
- **Cover Photo:** Nice property photo or professional headshot
- **Property Photos:** Add photos of your listings
- **Interior Photos:** If you have office photos

**Tip:** Add at least 10-20 photos for best results

#### **D. Services**
Add these services:
- ✅ Buying Properties

### **STEP 6: Create Your First Post**

1. Click **"Posts"** in the left menu
2. Click **"Add post"**
3. Choose **"What's new"** or **"Update"**
4. Write a post about:
   - New listings
   - Market updates
   - Tips for buyers/sellers
   - Neighborhood highlights

**Example Post:**
```

Beautiful customizable home available in Reunion Resort! Perfect for families looking for luxury living in Central Florida.

✨ 4 bedrooms, 3 bathrooms
✨ Customizable finishes
✨ Resort amenities included

Contact me to schedule a viewing!
(954) 303-3456

#OrlandoRealEstate #ReunionResort #CustomizableHomes
```

5. Add a photo
6. Click **"Publish"**

**Tip:** Post at least once per week for best results

---

### **STEP 7: Enable Messaging (Optional but Recommended)**

1. Go to **"Messages"** in left menu
2. Enable messaging
3. Set up quick replies:

## 📊 **MONITORING YOUR PROFILE**

Check your Google Business Profile regularly:
- **Views:** How many people saw your profile
- **Searches:** What people searched to find you
- **Actions:** Calls, website clicks, directions requests
- **Reviews:** New reviews and ratings

**Access:** Go to business.google.com and click "Performance"

---

## 🆘 **TROUBLESHOOTING**

**Problem:** Can't verify business
- **Solution:** Try phone or email verification instead of postcard

**Problem:** Business doesn't show up in search
- **Solution:** Wait 24-48 hours after verification. Make sure profile is 100% complete.

**Problem:** Wrong information showing
- **Solution:** Edit your profile and wait for changes to update (can take a few days)

**Problem:** Duplicate listing
- **Solution:** Claim the duplicate listing and merge it with your main one
