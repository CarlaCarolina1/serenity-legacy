## 🚀 **ADDITIONAL SEO STRATEGIES (RECOMMENDED)**

### **1. Google Business Profile** ⭐ **HIGH PRIORITY**
**What it is:** Free Google listing for your business  
**Why it matters:** Shows up in Google Maps and local search results  
**How to do it:**
1. Go to https://business.google.com
2. Create/claim your business listing
3. Add: Name, address, phone, website, hours, photos
4. Get verified (Google sends postcard or calls)
5. Add regular posts about new listings

**Impact:** Can appear in "Local Pack" (top 3 results) for local searches

---

### **2. Google Search Console** ⭐ **HIGH PRIORITY**
**What it is:** Free tool to see how Google sees your website  
**Why it matters:** Shows search performance, errors, indexing status  
**How to do it:**
1. Go to https://search.google.com/search-console
2. Add your property (serenitylegacy.net)
3. Verify ownership (HTML file or DNS)
4. Submit your sitemap: `https://serenitylegacy.net/sitemap.xml`
5. Monitor performance weekly

**Impact:** Helps Google index your site faster, shows what's working
